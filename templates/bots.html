The bots of TPAD

Derpy

Derpy is a Python IRC bot, originally created by sirtophat of PonyChat, but now maintained by AppleDash and ran on Canternet by him.
Derpy has many features, the most notable of which being her markov-like chat functionality; you may speak to her by addressing her with "derpy, some words" or "derpy, a question?". She also has many commands, a list of which can be viewd by typing !derpy in chat.

Chance

Chance is an eggdrop bot running the bMotion script. Not really much I can explain here, he has no commands, you really just need to figure him out for yourself. He is ran by AppleDash.

PinkiePieBot

PinkiePieBot is a bot by Kinsley. Nobody actually knows what she does, but that's alright beause she's fun anyways.

BurpyHooves

BurpyHooves is a bot by GyroTech, originally intended for a vore channel, but also joined to TPAD for some reason.
